Code of Conduct
Our Commitment
The DubbiOvi community is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of expertise.
We believe that open scientific software grows through collaboration, mutual respect, and constructive dialogue.
 
Our Values
As members of the DubbiOvi community, we strive to:
•	Treat everyone with courtesy and professionalism.
•	Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences.
•	Share knowledge openly.
•	Provide constructive feedback.
•	Support new contributors.
•	Foster collaboration across disciplines and cultures.
•	Promote ethical and responsible use of Artificial Intelligence.
 
Expected Behaviour
Examples of positive behaviour include:
•	Being respectful and considerate.
•	Accepting constructive criticism gracefully.
•	Focusing discussions on ideas rather than individuals.
•	Helping others learn and contribute.
•	Using inclusive and professional language.
•	Respecting cultural and linguistic diversity.
 
Unacceptable Behaviour
The following behaviours are not acceptable within the DubbiOvi community:
•	Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
•	Personal attacks or insults.
•	Offensive, abusive, or threatening language.
•	Deliberate intimidation.
•	Publishing private information without permission.
•	Trolling or intentionally disrupting discussions.
•	Any behaviour that undermines a respectful and collaborative environment.
 
Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:
•	GitHub repositories;
•	GitHub Issues;
•	Pull Requests;
•	Discussions;
•	project documentation;
•	community communications;
•	workshops and presentations related to DubbiOvi.
 
Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for promoting and enforcing this Code of Conduct.
When inappropriate behaviour is identified, maintainers may:
•	request changes in behaviour;
•	moderate discussions;
•	remove inappropriate content;
•	restrict participation when necessary.
Any action will be taken fairly, proportionately, and with the aim of maintaining a respectful community.

Our Commitment to Open Science
DubbiOvi is more than software.
It is an academic initiative that promotes:
•	open science;
•	open-source software;
•	responsible AI;
•	educational innovation;
•	reproducible research;
•	international collaboration.
Every contribution should support these principles.
 
Thank You
Thank you for helping create a respectful, collaborative, and welcoming community around DubbiOvi.
