Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 2026
1. Introduction
Welcome to DubbiOvi, an open-access audiovisual translation platform developed at the University of Oviedo (Spain).
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information is collected, why it is collected, how it is used, and the choices available to you.
By downloading or using DubbiOvi, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.
 
2. Data We Collect
When you voluntarily register to download DubbiOvi, we may collect the following information:
•	First name
•	Last name
•	Email address
•	Institution or organization
•	Country
•	Operating system (Windows or macOS)
•	Downloaded software version
•	Date and time of registration
No payment information is requested or stored.
 
3. Data We Do Not Collect
DubbiOvi has been designed following a local-first philosophy.
The application does not automatically collect or transmit:
•	videos
•	audio recordings
•	subtitles
•	translations
•	documents
•	projects
•	usage statistics
•	analytics
•	telemetry
•	keystrokes
•	screen recordings
Your work remains on your own computer unless you explicitly choose to use external AI services.
 
4. Use of External AI Services
Some optional features of DubbiOvi may interact with third-party Artificial Intelligence providers (for example, Google Gemini).
When these features are used:
•	only the information required to process your request is sent;
•	processing is performed according to the privacy policy of the selected provider;
•	the University of Oviedo does not store or monitor the information exchanged with those services.
Users are responsible for ensuring that they comply with their institution's data protection requirements when using external AI services.
 
5. Why We Collect Registration Data
Registration information is collected exclusively to:
•	understand the dissemination and impact of DubbiOvi;
•	communicate important software updates;
•	inform users about significant security notices;
•	produce anonymous aggregated statistics for research and knowledge transfer purposes;
•	improve future versions of the software.
Registration data are never sold or used for advertising purposes.
 
6. Data Storage
Registration information is stored securely using Google Workspace services managed by the project administrator.
Access is restricted to authorised project personnel.
Reasonable technical and organisational measures are implemented to protect the information.
 
7. Data Sharing
Personal information is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.
Aggregated and fully anonymised statistics (for example, number of downloads by country or institution type) may be used in academic publications, project reports, funding applications, or presentations.
 
8. Data Retention
Registration data are retained only for as long as necessary to support the DubbiOvi project, communicate important updates, and fulfil legitimate academic and research purposes.
Users may request deletion of their personal information at any time.
 
9. Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
•	access your personal information;
•	correct inaccurate information;
•	request deletion of your information;
•	restrict processing where applicable;
•	object to processing.
Requests can be sent using the contact details below.
